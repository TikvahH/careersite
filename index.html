TIPS

  • Question: How important is team building and team work?
    • Tip: The appropriate answer should reflect your ability to lead when necessary as well as knowing when to accept instructions. Answer: In the past, I have learned the importance of relying on each team member's strength to ensure successful completion of assigned projects and optimal results.
  • Question: What are the important rewards you expect from your employer?
    • Tip: Avoid discussing money, salary requirements, or pay structures. This is a test! Expound upon your desire to grow within the company and sharpen your skills and talents. Answer: It is important that I select a job, company and position that is a great fit for me by allowing me to grow. I receive satisfaction and accomplishment by exceeding expectations set by my superiors in a results driven environment.
  • Question: What has been your greatest professional disappointment? How did you overcome it?
    • Tip: This question gauges the applicant's maturity level, professional standards, values, and priorities. Answer: In my previous position, I was not granted the promotion that I applied for. I was greatly disappointed because I was confident in my abilities to lead and add value to my company. I decided to seek additional training, coaching and acquired a mentor in the related to field to ensure my skill set was solid.
  • Question: What determines your personal / professional choices?
    • Tip: Your response should reflect solid decision making skills with an developed approach to execution that has proven effective. Answer: I strongly feel that having a sense of balance is imperative in being productive and effective. To be highly functional in today's times requires time management and balance in every facet.

  • Question: Throughout your professional career, which accomplishments has given you the most satisfaction?
    • Tip: The interviewer is looking for an answer that illustrates commitments, diligence, and an high level of competence. Answer: In my current job, I was asked to fulfill the duties of my supervisor while he was away on business. I was relatively new to the assigned duties but I was able to quickly master the responsibilities with ease and comfort.
